FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Clear Answers.
No False Promises.

Trading Edge is not for everyone. These answers will help you decide if it’s right for you.

Is Trading Edge a signal service?

No. Trading Edge is a prop firm preparation programme. Some plans include trade guidance, but the focus is on structure, rules, discipline, and execution — not blind signals.

Do you guarantee funded accounts or profits?

Absolutely not. We do not offer guarantees. Prop firm success depends on discipline, execution, and adherence to rules. Trading Edge prepares you — outcomes depend on the trader.

Who is Trading Edge NOT for?

This programme is not for complete beginners, people looking for guaranteed income, or traders unwilling to control emotions and follow rules.

Which prop firms does this help with?

The preparation principles apply to most major prop firms. The focus is on rule awareness, drawdown management, and consistency — not a specific firm.

What if I still fail my challenge?

Failure is part of trading. Trading Edge helps reduce mistakes, improve discipline, and build a repeatable process — not eliminate risk entirely.

Is this legal?

Yes. Trading Edge is an educational and mentorship-based programme. We do not handle funds or offer investment advice.

How do I join?

Applications are handled via WhatsApp. Some programmes may have limited seats or screening.
