Why Most Traders Fail Prop Firms

No Rule Awareness

Most traders violate drawdown, consistency, or daily loss rules without even realising it.

Emotional Execution

Good strategies fail when fear, revenge trading, and overconfidence take control.

Lack of Structure

Random position sizing, no journaling, no review process — prop firms punish inconsistency.
